\( \require{cancel} \newcommand{\d}[2]{\frac{d #1}{d #2}} \newcommand{\dt}[1]{\d{#1}{t}} \newcommand{\pd}[2]{\frac{\partial #1}{\partial #2}} \newcommand{\pdt}[1]{\pd{#1}{t}} \newcommand{\hdiv}{\nabla_{\pi} \cdot \mathbf{v}} \newcommand{\cpocv}{\frac{C_p}{C_V}} \newcommand{\cvocp}{\frac{C_V}{C_p}} \)

静力气压坐标

采用基于静力气压或质量坐标的预报方程组（Laprise 1992） \[ \begin{align} \dt{\mathbf{v}} & = - \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla_\pi p - \pd{p}{\pi} \nabla_\pi \phi + \mathbf{F} \\ {\color{red}\gamma \dt{w}} & {\color{red}= g \left( \pd{p}{\pi} - 1 \right) + \gamma F_z} \\ \dt{\theta} & = \frac{Q}{C_p} \frac{\theta}{T} \\ \pdt{\pi_{es}} & = - \int_{\pi_t}^{\pi_s} \nabla_\pi \cdot \mathbf{v} d \pi \\ {\color{red}\dt{\phi}} & {\color{red}= w g} \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}w = \frac{1}{g} \dt{\phi}} \end{align} \] 其中\(\pi\)为静力气压，地面有效静力气压\(\pi_{es} = \pi_s - \pi_t\)，\(\pi_s\)为静力地面气压，\(\pi_t\)为模式顶气压，\(\gamma = 0\)时表明采用静力平衡假设。上面的方程组中红色项在非静力模式里计算，蓝色项在静力模式里计算。 若干诊断方程

  • 连续方程（计算静力气压垂直速度\(\dot{\pi}\)） \[ \hdiv + \pd{\dot{\pi}}{\pi} = 0 \to \dot{\pi} = - \int_{\pi_t}^{\pi} \hdiv d \pi \]
  • 静力方程（非静力模式计算密度\(\rho\)，静力模式计算位势\(\phi\)） \[ {\color{red}\rho = - \left( \pd{\phi}{\pi} \right)^{-1}} \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}\pd{\phi}{\pi} = - \frac{1}{\rho}} \]
  • 代入位温定义式的状态方程（非静力模式计算气压，静力模式计算密度） \[ {\color{red}p = p_0 \left( \frac{\rho R \theta}{p_0} \right)^{\cpocv}} \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}\rho = \frac{p_0}{R \theta} \left( \frac{p}{p_0} \right)^{\cvocp}} \]
若干定义式
  • 时间全导数 \[ \dt{} = \hdiv + \dot{\pi} \pd{}{\pi} \]
  • 三维散度 \[ D_3 = \hdiv + \rho \nabla_{\pi} \phi \cdot \pd{\mathbf{v}}{\pi} - \rho g \pd{w}{\pi} \]
  • 位温 \[ \theta = T \left( \frac{p_0}{p} \right)^{\frac{R}{C_p}} \]

广义地形跟随坐标

引入一个关于静力气压坐标\(\pi\)的单调函数\(\eta \left( \pi, \pi_s \right)\)，满足如下地形追随条件：

  • 模式底是坐标面1：\(\eta \left( \pi_s, \pi_s \right) = 1\)
  • 模式顶是坐标面0：\(\eta \left( \pi_t, \pi_s \right) = 0\)
  • 上下边界的垂直坐标速度为零：\(\dot{\eta} \left( \pi_s, \pi_s \right) = \dot{\eta} \left( \pi_t, \pi_s \right) = 0\)
坐标转换式 \[ \begin{align} \left( \pd{F}{t} \right)_\pi & = \left( \pd{F}{t} \right)_\eta - \pd{\eta}{\pi} \left( \pdt{\pi} \right)_\eta \pd{F}{\eta} \\ \nabla_\pi F & = \nabla_\eta F - \pd{\eta}{\pi} \nabla_\eta \pi \pd{F}{\eta} \end{align} \]

  • 连续方程 \[ \begin{equation} \pd{}{\eta} \pdt{\pi} + \nabla_{\eta} \cdot \left( \mathbf{v} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) + \pd{}{\eta} \left( \dot{\eta} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) = 0 \label{eq:eta-continuity-equation} \end{equation} \]
  • 静力方程 \[ \begin{equation} \pd{\phi}{\eta} = - \frac{1}{\rho} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \label{eq:eta-hydrostatic-equation} \end{equation} \]
  • 动量方程 \[ \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla_\pi p = \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla_\eta p - \cancel{\frac{1}{\rho} \pd{\eta}{\pi} \pd{p}{\eta} \nabla_\eta \pi} \] \[ \begin{align*} \pd{p}{\pi} \nabla_{\pi} \phi & = \pd{p}{\pi} \nabla_{\eta} \phi - \pd{p}{\pi} \pd{\eta}{\pi} \pd{\phi}{\eta} \nabla_\eta \pi \\ & = \pd{p}{\pi} \nabla_{\eta} \phi + \pd{p}{\pi} \pd{\eta}{\pi} \frac{1}{\rho} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \nabla_\eta \pi \quad\leftarrow\text{代入静力方程\eqref{eq:eta-hydrostatic-equation}} \\ & = \pd{p}{\pi} \nabla_{\eta} \phi + \cancel{\pd{p}{\pi} \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla_\eta \pi} \end{align*} \] 上面两式中的第二项相消，因此动量方程形式上仅变换水平梯度算子的下标 \[ \begin{equation} \dt{\mathbf{v}} = - \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla_{\eta} p - \pd{p}{\pi} \nabla_{\eta} \phi + \mathbf{F} \end{equation} \]
  • 地面有效静力气压方程 对\eqref{eq:eta-continuity-equation}两侧做\(\eta\)从\(0\)到\(1\)的积分，其中应用了在上下边界上\(\dot{\eta} = 0\)的条件 \[ \pdt{\pi_{es}} = - \int_0^1 \nabla_\eta \cdot \left( \mathbf{v} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) d \eta \]

广义地形追随坐标系下的预报方程组为 \[ \begin{align} \dt{\mathbf{v}} & = - \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla_{\eta} p - \pd{p}{\eta} \pd{\eta}{\pi} \nabla_{\eta} \phi + \mathbf{F} \\ {\color{red}\gamma \dt{w}} & {\color{red}= g \left( \pd{p}{\eta} \pd{\eta}{\pi} - 1 \right) + \gamma F_z} \\ \dt{\theta} & = \frac{Q}{C_p} \frac{\theta}{T} \\ \pdt{\pi_{es}} & = - \int_0^1 \nabla_\eta \cdot \left( \mathbf{v} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) d \eta \\ {\color{red}\dt{\phi}} & {\color{red}= w g} \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}w = \frac{1}{g} \dt{\phi}} \end{align} \] 诊断方程为 \[ \begin{align} \pd{}{\eta} \pdt{\pi} + \nabla_{\eta} \cdot \left( \mathbf{v} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) + \pd{}{\eta} \left( \dot{\eta} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) & = 0 \to \dot{\eta} \pd{\pi}{\eta} = - \pdt{\pi} - \int_0^\eta \nabla_{\eta} \cdot \left( \mathbf{v} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) d \eta \\ {\color{red}\rho = - \pd{\pi}{\eta} \left( \pd{\phi}{\eta} \right)^{-1}} & \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}\pd{\phi}{\eta} = - \frac{1}{\rho} \pd{\pi}{\eta}} \\ {\color{red}p = p_0 \left( \frac{\rho R \theta}{p_0} \right)^{\cpocv}} & \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}\rho = \frac{p_0}{R \theta} \left( \frac{p}{p_0} \right)^{\cvocp}} \end{align} \] 其中\(\pd{p}{\eta}\)和\(\pd{\pi}{\eta}\)来自于坐标定义，红色项是非静力模式需要计算的项，蓝色为静力模式。

几种特定形式

混合坐标

\[ \begin{equation} \pi( x, y, \eta, t) = A(\eta) + B(\eta) \pi_s(x, y, t) \label{eq:hybrid-coordinate} \end{equation} \] 其中函数\(A(\eta)\)和\(B(\eta)\)满足如下条件：1）\(A(1) = 0, B(1) = 1\)；2）\(A(0) = \pi_t, B(0) = 0\)。对\eqref{eq:hybrid-coordinate}做时间、\(\eta\)和水平坐标的导数得到 \[ \begin{align*} \pdt{\pi} & = B \pdt{\pi_s} \\ \pd{\pi}{\eta} & = \d{A}{\eta} + \pi_s \d{B}{\eta} \\ \nabla_\eta \pi & = B \nabla_\eta \pi_s = B \pi_s \nabla_\eta \ln \pi_s \end{align*} \]

平缓坐标（SLEVE）

\[ \begin{equation} \pi(x, y, \eta, t) = \pi_{es}^* \eta + \left[ \pi_{sm}^*(x,y) - \pi_{es}^* \right] \eta^\alpha + \left[ \pi_{es}(x,y,t) - \pi_{sm}^*(x,y) \right] \eta^\beta + \pi_t \end{equation} \] 其中\(\pi_{es}^*\)、\(\alpha\)和\(\beta\)是可调参数，\(\pi_{sm}^*\)是经过光滑的\(\pi_{es}\)。 \[ \begin{align*} \pdt{\pi} & = \eta^\beta \pdt{\pi_s} \\ \pd{\pi}{\eta} & = \pi_{es}^* + \alpha \left[ \pi_{sm}^* - \pi_{es}^* \right] \eta^{\alpha - 1} + \beta \left[ \pi_{es} - \pi_{sm}^* \right] \eta^{\beta - 1} \\ \nabla_\eta \pi & = \left( \eta^\alpha - \eta^\beta \right) \nabla_\eta \pi_{sm}^* + \eta^\beta \nabla_\eta \pi_s \end{align*} \]

扰动形式

为了减少离散计算时的截断误差（大项小差问题参见这里），将方程写为扰动形式。设置静力气压坐标系下的扰动态 \[ \begin{align} p^\prime(x, y, \overline{\pi}, t) & = p(x, y, \overline{\pi}, t) - \overline{\pi} \\ \theta^\prime(x, y, \overline{\pi}, t) & = \theta(x, y, \overline{\pi}, t) - \overline{\theta}(\overline{\pi}) \\ \phi^\prime(x, y, \overline{\pi}, t) & = \phi(x, y, \overline{\pi}, t) - \overline{\phi}(\overline{\pi}) \\ \pi_{es}^\prime(x, y) & = \pi_{es}(x, y) - \overline{\pi}_{es}(x, y) \end{align} \] 其中基准静力气压\(\overline{\pi}\)的存在是由于采用了基准地面静力气压\(\overline{\pi}_{es}\)。由于\(\eta\)坐标面相对于\(\pi\)不是“水平”的，因此基本态\(\overline{\pi}, \overline{\theta}, \overline{\phi}\)是\(x, y, \eta\)的函数。代入以上扰动后，预报方程组变为如下形式（推导） \[ \begin{align} \dt{\mathbf{v}} & = {\color{purple}- \left( \alpha - \pd{p}{\eta} \pd{\eta}{\pi} \overline{\alpha} \right) \nabla_\eta \overline{\pi}} - \alpha \nabla_\eta p^\prime - \pd{p}{\eta} \pd{\eta}{\pi} \nabla_\eta \phi^\prime + \mathbf{F} \\ {\color{red}\gamma \dt{w}} & {\color{red}= g \left( \pd{p^\prime}{\eta} \pd{\eta}{\pi} + \pd{\overline{\pi}}{\eta} \pd{\eta}{\pi} - 1\right) + \gamma F_z} \\ \dt{\theta^\prime} & = - \d{\overline{\theta}}{\overline{\pi}} \left( {\color{purple}\mathbf{v} \cdot \nabla_\eta \overline{\pi}} + \dot{\eta} \pd{\overline{\pi}}{\eta} \right) + \frac{Q}{C_p} \frac{\theta}{T} \\ \pdt{\pi_{es}^\prime} & = - \int_0^1 \nabla_\eta \cdot \left( \mathbf{v} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) d \eta \\ {\color{red}\dt{\phi^\prime}} & {\color{red}= - \d{\overline{\phi}}{\overline{\pi}} \left( {\color{purple}\mathbf{v} \cdot \nabla_\eta \overline{\pi}} + \dot{\eta} \pd{\overline{\pi}}{\eta} \right) + w g} \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}w = \frac{1}{g} \dt{\phi^\prime} + \frac{1}{g} \d{\overline{\phi}}{\overline{\pi}} \left( {\color{purple}\mathbf{v} \cdot \nabla_\eta \overline{\pi}} + \dot{\eta} \pd{\overline{\pi}}{\eta} \right)} \end{align} \] 诊断方程 \[ \begin{align} \dot{\eta} \pd{\pi}{\eta} & = - \pdt{\pi} - \int_0^\eta \nabla_{\eta} \cdot \left( \mathbf{v} \pd{\pi}{\eta} \right) d \eta \\ {\color{red}\pd{\phi^\prime}{\eta} = - \pd{\overline{\pi}}{\eta} \alpha^\prime - \pd{\pi^\prime}{\eta} \alpha} & \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}\pd{\phi^\prime}{\eta} = - \pd{\overline{\pi}}{\eta} \alpha^\prime - \pd{\pi^\prime}{\eta} \alpha} \\ {\color{red}p = p_0 \left( \frac{\rho R \theta}{p_0} \right)^{\cpocv}} & \quad \text{或} \quad {\color{blue}\rho = \frac{p_0}{R \theta} \left( \frac{p}{p_0} \right)^{\cvocp}} \end{align} \]
